Large Diameter Pipe Runs

For commercial and larger diameter drains we utilise our crawler pan and tilt cameras. These cameras will survey from 150mm - 1500mm diameter pipes and can handle runs of up to 200m in length.

With the use of their high intensity LED lighting and robotic camera head, we can ensure excellent picture quality with great detail and definition; allowing us to identify any faults that are present in the run.

Our highly trained and experienced survey technicians have a helpful can-do attitude; coupled with the most up to date equipment we guarantee a first rate drainage survey and report for you and your company. Along with the written reports we also provide a full colour DVD of the camera footage and a sketch of the drainage layout for your reference. All work is carried out to Water Authority standards.

  • Colour DVD and written report included
  • Highly trained technicians
