Procedure

Our office will give you a fixed price for your drain survey and then will make a mutually convenient appointment with you, morning or afternoon to carry out the work. Weekend appointments are also available to help fit in with busy lives.

When our engineers arrive they will feed a camera head attached to a cable or reel into your drain. The camera head has a bright light incorporated in its structure which helps produce sharp images that are transmitted back to the monitor. Meter by meter we record your drainage system and we note any defects on your written report. Your written report will also include a site plan of your drainage system showing flow direction, depth and manhole position. When your drain survey has been completed you will receive a colour DVD recording along with a full written report and any recommendations.

All the work undertaken by Drain Surveyor London is carried out in a professional, friendly way, whatever service you need such as drain clearing/cleaning/unblocking, CCTV drain surveys, high pressure water jetting, drain repairs, gease traps etc.
