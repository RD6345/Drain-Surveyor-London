Things We Can Find

During a drainage survey, we can find some of these things: tree root ingress; redundant drain lines; displaced joints; partial and total collapse; rodent infestation (usually from redundant drain lines); high build ups of fat and grease; lime scale. We will give a recommendation on any of these things found.
